Yes — joining FlexWork costs nothing. There are no registration fees, membership charges, or hidden costs at any point.
How and when do I get paid?
Earnings are credited to your account after task completion. You can request a withdrawal to your preferred payment method at any time once you hit the minimum threshold.
Can students and part-time workers join?
Absolutely. FlexWork is designed around flexible schedules. Students, homemakers, and working professionals all participate alongside each other.
How many tasks can I complete per day?
There is no hard cap. Tasks are refreshed daily and availability depends on current campaigns. Most members complete between 5 and 20 tasks on an active day.
